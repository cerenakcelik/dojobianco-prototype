Embodied Presence — Returning to the Living Body

Mind–body connection and emotional attunement.

Presence is not achieved by effort; it emerges when the mind relaxes into the body’s truth. We practice coming home to sensation, emotion, and breath — as they are — without forcing change. This is the ground of honest contact.

Self-assessment
  • Mind–body: Thoughts / Moving between / With sensation
  • Emotion: Clear / Muted / Overwhelmed
  • Contact: Narrow / Balanced / Wide (add one anchor)
Practice (5 minutes)
  1. Sense map: 3 sensations (pressure, temperature, movement).
  2. Emotion check: joy, sadness, fear, anger, calm, not sure.
  3. Breath bridge: let feeling be held by breath, not mind.
  4. Include one external sound and the support beneath you.
“Presence is not achieved. It is remembered — each time we meet ourselves in honesty.” — Dojo Bianco Teaching
