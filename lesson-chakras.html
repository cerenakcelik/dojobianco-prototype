Chakras & Energy — A Calm Introduction

Brennan-inspired: chakras as transformers between body and subtle fields — practical, experiential, grounded.

The human energy field is the bridge between consciousness and matter. Chakras organize how life moves through you as warmth, vibration, expansion, and meaning.

Seven Centers
  • Root — safety & belonging · feel soles and base of spine.
  • Sacral — flow & pleasure · belly breath, gentle pelvis mobility.
  • Solar Plexus — will & clarity · warmth around navel/ribs.
  • Heart — love & balance · breathe behind the sternum.
  • Throat — truth & expression · hum softly; relax jaw.
  • Brow — insight & perspective · space behind eyes.
  • Crown — connection & meaning · awareness up, return to heart.
Practice — Vertical Alignment (7 min)

Sense a central column of light (hara). Breathe crown → root; exhale through the feet. Let each center harmonize. Rest awareness at the heart.
